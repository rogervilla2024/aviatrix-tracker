How to Play Aviatrix: NFT Aircraft Guide

Published: January 15, 2026 Updated: January 17, 2026 Reading time: 7 min

Aviatrix is an innovative crash game that combines simple gameplay mechanics with a unique NFT aircraft collection system. This comprehensive guide will teach you everything you need to know about playing Aviatrix, from basic controls to understanding the NFT features.

Quick Game Facts

  • RTP: 97% (3% house edge)
  • Max Multiplier: 10,000x
  • Special Feature: NFT aircraft collection system
  • Developer: Aviatrix.bet

Basic Gameplay: Step by Step

Playing Aviatrix is straightforward. Here's a complete walkthrough of how each round works:

1

Place Your Bet

Before each round begins, you have a betting window to place your wager. Enter your desired bet amount using the interface. Most versions allow you to place up to two simultaneous bets per round, enabling different cash-out strategies.

2

Watch the Aircraft Take Off

Once the betting period closes, your NFT aircraft (or default aircraft) takes off. The multiplier starts at 1.00x and begins increasing. The speed of increase varies, creating tension as you decide when to cash out.

3

Cash Out at the Right Moment

Click the "Cash Out" button at any time while the aircraft is still flying. Your bet is multiplied by the current multiplier value. The longer you wait, the higher the potential payout - but also the higher the risk of losing everything.

4

Win or Lose

If you cash out before the aircraft flies away (crashes), you win your bet multiplied by the cash-out multiplier. If the aircraft crashes before you cash out, you lose your entire bet for that round.

  • History: Previous round results showing crash points

Auto Cash-Out Feature

The auto cash-out feature allows you to set a predetermined multiplier at which your bet will automatically cash out. This is useful for:

  • Removing emotional decision-making from the cash-out moment
  • Ensuring you don't miss your target due to slow reactions
  • Implementing consistent strategies

Auto Cash-Out Limitations

Auto cash-out only works if the game reaches your target multiplier. If the aircraft crashes at 1.85x and your auto cash-out is set to 2.00x, you will still lose your bet. Auto cash-out does not guarantee wins.

Betting Strategies Overview

💲

⚖

🔄

Important Strategy Disclaimer

No strategy can guarantee profits or overcome the 3% house edge. All betting strategies are ways to manage your gameplay experience, not methods to beat the game. Over time, the mathematical edge always favors the house.

Tips for New Players

  1. Start with small bets: Learn the game mechanics before wagering larger amounts
  2. Use auto cash-out: Remove emotion from your decisions
  3. Set strict limits: Decide your budget before playing and stick to it
  4. Don't chase losses: If you lose, don't increase bets to recover
  5. Understand the odds: Remember that ~48.5% of rounds crash below 2x
  6. Play for entertainment: Treat gambling as a form of entertainment, not income

Common Mistakes to Avoid

  • Waiting too long: Greed often leads to losses when players wait for just "a bit more"
  • Believing in patterns: Each round is independent - past results don't predict future outcomes
  • Chasing losses: Increasing bets after losses is a fast way to deplete your bankroll
  • Ignoring limits: Always set and respect your gambling budget
  • Emotional decisions: Making bets based on frustration or excitement rarely ends well
